Privacy Policy for Hexa Merge - Color Puzzle

Effective Date: December 14, 2024
Last Updated: December 14, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Hexa Merge - Color Puzzle ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Important: By using Hexa Merge - Color Puzzle, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Game Progress: Your game scores, level progression, unlocked items, and achievements are stored locally on your device.
  • Settings & Preferences: Your in-game settings such as sound preferences, haptic feedback settings, and selected themes.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including gameplay sessions, features used, and in-app events.
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and performance diagnostics to improve app stability.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements (see Advertising section below)
  • Google Play Services: For in-app purchases and app functionality
  • Firebase Analytics: For understanding app usage and improving user experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To save your game progress and settings
  • To process in-app purchases
  • To display personalized advertisements
  • To analyze app usage and improve user experience
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • To send important updates about the App (if you opt-in)

3. Advertising

Hexa Merge displays advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Show you personalized ads based on your interests
  • Measure ad performance and engagement
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Ad Personalization

You can control ad personalization through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

Learn more about Google's advertising practices: Google Ads Policy

4. In-App Purchases

Hexa Merge offers in-app purchases including:

  • Remove ads
  • Cosmetic items (skins and themes)
  • In-game currency

All purchases are processed through Google Play Store's payment system. We do not collect or store your payment information. Please review Google Play's privacy policy for information about how payment data is handled.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Game progress, settings, and purchased items are stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage.
  • Cloud Storage: We do not currently store your data on our servers.
  • Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Hexa Merge is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

COPPA Compliance: We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Our app:

  • Does not require account creation
  • Does not collect personal information from users
  • Uses family-friendly advertising practices
  • Does not include social features or chat functionality

7. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as our social media pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Data Retention

  • Game Data: Stored locally until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Analytics Data: Retained by third-party services according to their policies (typically 14-26 months)
  • Advertising Data: Managed by AdMob according to Google's data retention policies

9. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about the data we collect
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Opt-out: Disable ad personalization in your device settings
  • Restore Purchases: Restore your in-app purchases on a new device

How to Delete Your Data

To completely remove your data:

  1. Go to your device Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps > Hexa Merge
  3. Tap "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage"
  4. Uninstall the app
